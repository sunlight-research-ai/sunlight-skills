# Track 3 Compressed Findings: Business Model, Governance, and Risk

- Figma has evidence of business momentum and AI-credit monetization, which supports a strategy that combines collaboration seats, usage-based AI, and enterprise controls. [SRC_007] [SRC_008]
- Governance is not optional for AI-assisted product workflows because MCP and agent execution patterns introduce permission, context, and integration-boundary risks. [SRC_009] [SRC_011] [SRC_012]
